# v0.0.2

Starshelf now lets you control how your star lists are named — emojis, category prefixes, or whatever style you prefer. The new auto-format mode even detects your existing naming conventions and matches them automatically.

## Highlights

- **You control the formatting** — Toggle emoji prefixes and `Category: Name` formatting on or off from the popup settings
- **Auto-detect your style** — With auto-format enabled, Starshelf reads your existing star lists and matches their naming convention (emojis, category prefixes, or plain text)
- **Works across all AI providers** — Formatting preferences are passed to Anthropic, OpenAI, and OpenCode alike

## Notes

Auto-format is enabled by default. When on, the manual emoji and category prefix toggles are disabled — Starshelf automatically follows whatever style it detects in your existing lists. Turn auto-format off to force your preferred style regardless of past list names.
